Fettucini

Description

Fettucini is a simple Italian dish consisting of long flat noodles and a sauce made by reducing cream and adding parmesan cheese.

To make fettucini, simple cook noodles while reducing cream in a seperate pan, when sufficiently reduced, add parmesan cheese and drained noodles.

Ingredients

  • Fettucini noodles
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring salted pot of water to a boil
  2. Boil noodles in water for duration indicated on noodle packaging
  3. While noodles cook, reduce heavy cream in pan over medium-low heat until it coats the back of a spoon
  4. After cream has reduced, add parmesan cheese and remove from heat
  5. Drain noodles and add them to cream sauce
  6. Vigorously combine noodles and cream sauce
  7. Serve immediately
