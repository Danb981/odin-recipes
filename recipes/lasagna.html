Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish consisting of flat sheets of noodles, tomato sauce, and cheese, but other additions such as meat and vegetables are common.

To make lasagna, you arrange the noodles in a dish in layers alternating between noodles, sauce and cheese. Once assembled, it is cooked in an oven for up to an hour.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • (Optional) Meat
  • (Optional) Vegetables

Steps

  1. Bring salted pot of water to a boil
  2. Boil noodles in water for duration indicated on noodle packaging
  3. Drain noodles
  4. Add noodles, sauce, and cheese to dish in alternating layers
  5. Bake in oven at 375 degrees F for 30 minutes
  6. Rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving
