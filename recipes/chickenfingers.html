Chicken Fingers

Description

Chicken fingers are a dish of breaded chicken tenders/fillets that are fried or baked.

To make chicken fingers, coat chicken tenders in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, then fry or bake.

Ingredients

  • Chicken tenders
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Coat chicken tenders in flour
  2. Coat floured chicken tenders in egg
  3. Coat floured and egged chicken tenders in bread crumbs
  4. Arrange coated chicken tenders on a parchment lined baking sheet
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes
  6. Remove from oven and serve immediately
